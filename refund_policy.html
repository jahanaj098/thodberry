Refund Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

At Thodberry, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction across all our ventures and services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be granted.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refund requests will be considered on a case-by-case basis and must be raised within 7 days of purchase, unless otherwise stated for a specific product or service.

2. Non-Refundable Items

Certain services, including but not limited to consulting, custom development work, and digital products already delivered or accessed, are non-refundable.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact us at contact@thodberry.com with your order details and reason for the request. Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

4. Cancellations

Cancellation requests must be submitted before the service or product has been delivered or work has commenced, in order to be eligible for a full refund.

5. Changes to This Policy

Thodberry reserves the right to amend this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact Us

For refund-related queries, reach out to contact@thodberry.com.
